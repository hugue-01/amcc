Who We Are

Rwanda's Premier Construction Partner

Affordable Modern Construction Company (AMCC) has been transforming Rwanda's built environment since 2015. We specialize in delivering high-quality residential and commercial structures that combine contemporary design with structural integrity.

From small family homes to large commercial complexes, we bring the same dedication, expertise, and craftsmanship to every project. Our team of certified engineers, architects, and skilled workers ensure every build exceeds your expectations.

Permits & Legal

We Handle All Permits

Navigating Rwanda's building permit process can be complex and time-consuming. Our dedicated permits team handles everything from start to finish, liaising with the Rwanda Building Authority, REMA, and all relevant municipal offices on your behalf.

With years of experience processing permits across all districts, we get approvals faster and with fewer delays — so your construction can start on schedule.
